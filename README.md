### About me

I'm a Petroleum Engineer with a strong foundation in **mathematical modeling, numerical simulation, data analysis, and process optimization**. That background taught me something that now guides all my work: physical simulation models use equations to represent a system's behavior; AI models learn those same patterns directly from data. That connection was the starting point of my transition into **conversational Artificial Intelligence**.

Today I focus my career on **Conversational AI, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Large Language Models (LLMs)**, with the goal of designing systems that combine language models, knowledge retrieval (RAG), intelligent agents, and human-AI interaction to solve real problems.

### My purpose

I don't build AI for technology's sake. I want to **democratize access to specialized knowledge**, building intelligent assistants that let people and organizations access complex knowledge in a simple way. My core focus is **knowledge transfer**.
